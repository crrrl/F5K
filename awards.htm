Awards Ceremony

The Furry 5K awards ceremony takes place at 11:15 a.m. The ceremony honors the following:

  • Medals for overall first, second and third place male and female runners
  • Medals for first, second and third place dogs running with men
  • Medals for first, second and third place dogs running with women
  • Trophy and prizes for top fundraising team
  • Medal and prize for top fundraising individual

Entertainment

Immediately following the awards ceremony the entertainment begins! Watch specially trained dogs show off their amazing skills and stick around to enter your pet in our Totally Talented dog trick competition where you can win all sorts of prizes your for pet.
