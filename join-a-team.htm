Early Race Packet Pick-Up

Avoid the registration table crowds on race day by picking up your team race packets which include: t-shirt, bib number, goody bag and timing device (if you are being timed) on Saturday, June 9th. You can stop by the upper level of the Seattle Animal Shelter between 10 a.m. and 2 p.m. Please bring the e-mail confirmation you received when you registered your team or the list of all your team members.


All team captains are strongly encouraged to pick up their team members' race packets during this time. Please note that team captains are responsible for distributing the packets to their team members.


The Seattle Animal Shelter is located at 2061 15th Ave. West.
