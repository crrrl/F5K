2012 13th Annual Furry 5k FUN RUN & WALK

The Furry 5k raises money for the Help the Animals Fund (HTAF), which provides veterinary care and supplies for sick, injured or abused orphaned shelter animals. The Seattle Animal Shelter takes in nearly 8,000 animals annually. Of those, many receive the lifesaving care they need thanks to the generous donations of individuals and companies. The HTAF is an integral part of saving lives – with annual veterinary expenses of approximately $100,000, every dollar raised counts! To read about how the Help the Animals Fund saves lives, click here.


Did you know? If each race participant raised just $25 in donations, we could support six more months of vet care.


Donations and Pledges can be made using the online donation and pledge form.

NEW THIS YEAR! - TEAM DONATION AWARDS:

The team with the most donations on their team website by 11:59 p.m. on June 8 will receive a team trophy! The team will also be announced on the Furry 5K stage during the awards ceremony.

INDIVIDUAL DONATION AWARDS:

The individual fundraiser with the most donations on their website by 11:59 p.m. on June 8 will receive an individual medal and prize! The individual will be announced on the Furry 5K stage during the awards ceremony.
