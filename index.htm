2012 13th Annual Furry 5k FUN RUN & WALK


The 2012 Furry 5k will be held on Sunday, June 10, at 10 a.m. at Seattle's Seward Park. Join the fun as thousands of people run and walk with their dogs to raise money for the Seattle Animal Shelter’s Help the Animals Fund!


You can participate in one or more ways:


  • As a runner or walker: Register now online.
  • As a team: Create a team and raise donations.
  • As a donor: Submit a pledge.
  • As a sponsor: Our sponsors make the Furry 5K possible. Find out how to support the race.

Also join us in advance for some special registration parties where you not only can register for the race, but can learn some running tips, win prizes and meet the Furry 5K team!


  • Tuesday, May 22, at 6 p.m.:
    Shoe and gear clinic, Road Runner Sports - Green Lake
  • Wednesday, May 23, at 6 p.m.:
    Shoe and gear clinic, Road Runner Sports - Bellevue
  • Wednesday, May 30, at 5 p.m.:
    Happy hour, Norm's in Fremont

Hope to see you there!
