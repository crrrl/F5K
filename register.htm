Join us for The Seattle Animal Shelter’s 13th Annual Furry 5K Fun Run and Walk!

Sunday, June 10, 2012 in Seattle’s Seward Park (Get directions!)


Join the fun as thousands of people run and walk with their dogs to raise money for the Seattle Animal Shelter’s Help the Animals Fund!

Early / Pre-Registration

Two ways to register:
  1. Online: Pre-register online Please note that a processing fee applies.
  2. In-Person: You can register in-person at any of the four stores below from April 27 thru June 5 (close of business). Timing for the event is available for those interested runners.
    • Fleet Feet Sports (Capitol Hill)
    • Sound Sports (Downtown Seattle)
    • Super Jock 'n Jill (Greenlake)
    • The Footzone (Bellevue, Issaquah, Redmond)
  3. Register by Mail: Print, fill out, sign and mail in the online entry form and fee to:

    Seattle Animal Shelter/Furry 5K

    2061 15th Ave West

    Seattle, WA 98119


    Form must be postmarked by June 1, 2012.
    Make checks payable to:
    Help the Animals Fund/Furry 5K

Day of Race Registration

Register on race day, Sunday, June 10, at Seward Park from 8:15-9:45 a.m.; timed runners must be registered by 9:30 a.m. Day of race registrants will be required to fill out a separate registration form at the event.

Registration Fees

  • Early Registration: $25.00 per person with t-shirt / $20.00 per person without t-shirt
  • Day-of-Race Registration: $30.00 per person* Checks preferred/cash OK. Sorry, no credit cards. *Race t-shirt not guaranteed those registered after May 18

Race Packet & T-shirt Pick-Up

Pre-registered participants can pick up their race packets and t-shirts:

  • Early packet pick-up: Upper level of the Seattle Animal Shelter on Saturday, June 9, between 10 a.m.-2 p.m. All team captains are strongly encouraged to pick up their team members' race packets during this time. Please note that team captains are responsible for distributing the packets to their team members. Please bring a photo ID and/or the e-mail confirmation you received if you registered for the race online.
  • Race Day Packet pick-up: Seward Park on Sunday, June 10, between 8:15 a.m. - 9:45 a.m. Race packets will be filed by event activity (5K Run or 5K Walk) and by last name. Look for the large pre-registration tent!

The Seattle Animal Shelter is located at 2061 15th Ave. West.

Collect Donations

Registering is the first step in helping the animals. To make an even bigger impact, we encourage you collect donations from your family, friends and colleagues. The top individual and team fundraisers will even win prizes. If every participant collected just one $25 pledge, we’d raise another $75,000! Think of all the lives that could save.

Timing and Results

  • Timing and results by Milliseconds Timing
  • Results will be posted at the finish line and on this website
  • Walkers will not be timed

Safety Counts

  • No retractable leashes
  • Leashes no longer than 6 feet (Seattle Municipal Code)
  • No dogs under 6 months
  • No female dogs in heat
  • No aggressive dogs
  • Dogs must be on a leash at all times
  • All dogs must be current on vaccinations
